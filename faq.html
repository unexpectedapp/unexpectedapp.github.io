FAQ


What if I fail to get any notifications?
The problem can be solved by following steps,
1. Make sure you allow notifications for Unexpected, it's under Settings -> Notifications -> Unexpected
2. Logout and re-login to Unexpected.
If you still don't receive any notifications, please contact us at unexpectedapp@gmail.com and provide your account name.

What is "Public Wall" in Unexpected?
"Public Wall" is a place that lets you share your random moments with other Unexpected users. You can choose to post your random moments and also to "the Public Wall".

Is there an Android version of Unexpected?
Yes, it's on Google Play!


If you have any other questions, please contact us at unexpectedapp@gmail.com.
