A brand new way of sharing your life with others

Traditional social media tools like Twitter, Instagram or Snapchat, allow you and your friends to share life's special moments with each other. However, people choose when and where they post and you only see what they want you to see. But what if we do that in reverse, an app that spontaneously asks you to make a post? Then a quick snapshot of life is shared between you and your friends. Whether getting ready to go out, walking the dog, studying at the library or even watching your favorite tv show while laying on the bed, you and your friends can share a real part of your lives, the results can be ...unexpected.

How it works
Each day Unexpected will randomly pick a time for each user to make a post. After getting the notification, the user will have 5 minutes to take a photo and share it. Don't be worried if you miss a notification, you may get another chance to make a post that day.

It's that easy! When the Unexpected notification visits you unexpectedly, you capture what you are doing, whom you are with or what you see at that moment and share it with your friends.
