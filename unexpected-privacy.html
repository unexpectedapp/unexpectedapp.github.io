Unexpected Privacy Policy


Thank you for using Unexpected. Unexpected provides a fun and different way for you to sharing photos with your friends. This policy is to help you understand what information we collect and how do we use it.

1. Information we collect
Basic Account Information:
Information when you sign up for an account or use Unexpected. We will ask you to link your Facebook account to Unexpected, which give us permission to obtain information from the Facebook account including your Facebook profile photo and friends list. Make sure you check your settings or their privacy policies. The information we get from those services depends on that.

User Content:
This includes photos, stories content, comments and likes that you post to us and your followers and following list.

Searching other users on Unexpected:
User names are searchable by other users.

2. How do we use the information we collect
We use the information we collect to provide our product to you and make improvement, develop new version of our product, and protect our users and Unexpected.

3. Sharing of your information
We will not give your information to any third parties outside Unexpected.

4. Children
Unexpected is not directed to to anyone under 13. And that's why we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe that we might have any information from or about anyone under 13, please contact us.

5. Update of this privacy policy
We may change this policy from time to time, and we will update this page if we do so. We may provide you more prominent of notice of modifications or updates if the changes are significant. Your continued use of Unexpected after any modification or update to this Privacy Policy means that you agree to the revised policy.

If you have any questions or comments about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at unexpectedapp@gmail.com.
